My Top 3 Movies of all time.

Robocop

Robocop is the story of a Detroit Police officer who gets critically wounded on the job while attempting to arrest a local gang. Murphy fights for his live as he's saved by Omni Corp to turn him into the best police officer the robot has ever seen.

Terminator

Terminator is the story of Sarah Connor who learns from Reece, a soldier from the future that she is the savior of mankind and she must be protected at all costs so that she can give birth to the John Connor, the savior of humanity.

Kickboxer

This story is about a kickboxer who trains his hardest to become the best so he can beat his brother's opponent, an evil unscrupiouls Thai kickboxer who left him paralized from the waist down.
